Pumphouse Brewery Review

Thu 20 September 2012
By Adam (keflavich@gmail.com)

Went to the pumphouse brewery last night and 'tasted' 4 beers. First, for a brewpub, it's pretty weird to serve tastes in single-sip servings, but that's what they did. They didn't charge, so I didn't object, but it was difficult to get a strong sense for what the beers were meant to be. Beers: Cherry Saison: light colored, mild cherry flavor, no sweetness or depth to match the cherry flavor Ginger saison: strong ginger flavor, no depth or interesting beer flavor. Like a dry but strong ginger ale Saison: just... average Copper: Weak flavored reddish beer
