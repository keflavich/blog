ADS arXiv A mm and near-IR study of the IRAS 05358+3543 system. Recently (2011), it has become clear that "protocluster" is unlikely to be the right label for IRAS 05358+3543. "proto-association" might be more accurate. Perhaps the most interesting result of this paper is the discovery that the central source is likely to be a 400-AU binary with two massive (>8 msun) stars at different evolutionary stages.
